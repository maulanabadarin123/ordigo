Tokenomics

Tokens are crucial to any project. At Ordigo Protocol, we have a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 ORDIGO tokens that fund the Ordigo Protocol mission. For the cross-chain transfer, we need liquidity in most cases. This liquidity will be provided mainly by third parties. At the start, Ordigo Protocol will use its own liquidity. The project's success requires investors who see Ordigo as a long-term investment. Due to the fees' distribution structure, the Ordigo token is deflationary, as every month, we burn a percentage of the fee income in Ordigo tokens. For investors, this means further stability of your investment. Besides, users can stake ORDIGO tokens, for which the stakers receive a share of the profit from the fees. Furthermore, the percentage of staked tokens is used in the Government process when Ordigo asks all stakeholders to decide on voting, feature request, prioritization and changes. We believe that the decision of many is always better than that of a few. Also, Ordigo offers value-added services that we outlined briefly in the Swap chapter of this whitepaper. More such services will also emerge in the Search App space. These premium services will be paid for via ORDIGO tokens, offering everyone the opportunity to participate in these premium services at a low cost. For stakeholders, the premium services are offered at a lower price and can be paid automatically from the staking revenues.
