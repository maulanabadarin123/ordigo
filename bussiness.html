Bussiness

Ordigo Protocol provides the simplified exchange of tokens and coins at the current lowest price in the DeFi universe. This service will impose a small brokerage fee on each user, assessed on the total volume of the exchange.
Furthermore, Ordigo Protocol offers a subscription plan that reduces the fees for each swap. This plan is exciting, especially for active users. Besides, Ordigo Protoc offers further premium services in the subscription, which are paid accordingly in ORDIGO Tokens. Ordigo Protocol distributes fee profits to to the respective areas
Ordigo Protocol also acts as a liquidity provider in the market to establish optimal swap conditions. For the cross-network swaps, ORDIGO tokens or OLPs that OrdigoSwap must initially service to facilitate third-party entry.
Ordigo Protocol does not guarantee the value of the token launch. Users assume the risks in the token launch themselves. Users are deemed to have the necessary knowledge of the blockchain and undertake to participate in the Presale and to be financially literate.
With the DeBook OrdigoSwap establishes a platform that other participants can trade on. This provides a small fee to the project on any trade. OrdigoSwap will use the opportunities of the DeFi market itself to optimize profit through its platform. The trading strategies used will later be integrated into OrdigoSwap as premium services, offering users further trading opportunities.
